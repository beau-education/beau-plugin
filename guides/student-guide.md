# Student Guide

Welcome to the learning platform. This guide explains how to navigate your dashboard, access your courses, and track your progress.

## Your Dashboard

When you log in, you'll see your personalized learning dashboard with several sections:

### Overall Progress

At the top, you'll see a summary card showing:
- Your completion percentage across all enrolled courses
- The number of resources you've completed out of the total
- How many courses you're enrolled in

### Course Enrollments

This section shows all the courses you're enrolled in. For each course, you can see:
- Course name
- Your progress bar
- Enrollment status (Active, Paused, or Completed)
- When you started the course

Click on a course row to expand it and see details about each resource in that course.

### Course Catalog

Below your enrolled courses, you'll find the **Course Catalog** showing courses available for self-enrollment. These are open enrollment courses that you can join without needing to wait for a teacher invitation.

For each available course, you can see:
- Course name and description (click "Read more" for long descriptions)
- Price (displayed with proper currency symbol like £, $, €) or "Free" badge
- Tags indicating the subject area
- Number of resources in the course
- Enrollment capacity (if limited)
- The teacher who will support you

#### Enrolling in Free Courses

1. Browse the available courses in the catalog
2. Click **Enroll Now** on a course you're interested in
3. The course immediately appears in your **Course Enrollments** section
4. Click **Play** to start learning

#### Purchasing Paid Courses

Some courses require payment:

1. Browse courses - paid courses display their price (e.g., £29.99, $19.99)
2. Click **Purchase Course** to proceed
3. Complete payment on the secure Stripe checkout page
4. After payment, you're automatically enrolled
5. The course appears in your **Course Enrollments** section

**Note:** Some courses have enrollment limits. If a course shows "Course Full," you'll need to wait for a spot to open or contact your administrator.

### Resource Details

When you expand a course, you'll see each resource with:
- **Status**: Available, In Progress, Completed, or Locked
- **Attempts**: How many times you've attempted this resource
- **Score**: Your most recent score (if applicable)
- **Completed**: When you finished the resource

### Progress Charts

Your dashboard includes visual charts showing:
- Progress comparison across your enrolled courses
- A log of your recent activity

## Accessing Courses

### Playing a Course

To start learning:
1. Find a course you're enrolled in on your dashboard
2. Click the **Play** button on the course
3. The player will open in a new browser tab
4. The bot will guide you through the available resources in the course

The course's progression type determines which resources are available and in what order.

### Delivery Modes

Resources can be delivered in two different modes:

**Conversation Mode**:
- You speak with the AI tutor using your microphone
- Have a two-way voice conversation
- Ask questions and get immediate responses
- The tutor adapts to your pace and understanding

**Presentation Mode**:
- The AI tutor narrates the content like a lecture
- No microphone needed
- Click "Next" to advance through sections
- Great for listening and learning at your own pace

### Resource Progress

As you work through a course, each resource tracks your progress:
- **Available**: Ready for you to work on
- **In Progress**: You've started but not completed
- **Completed**: You've finished this resource
- **Locked**: Not yet available (complete earlier resources first in sequential courses)

## Understanding Course Progression

Courses can have different progression types:

- **Flexible**: Complete resources in any order you prefer
- **Sequential**: Resources must be completed in a specific order
- **Random**: The system selects which resource is available next

Your teacher sets the progression type when creating the course.

## Taking Quizzes

During lessons, the AI tutor may present quizzes to check your understanding. There are four types of quizzes:

### Choice Questions (Single or Multiple)

When you see answer options:
1. **Read the question** carefully
2. **Select your answer(s)** - some quizzes allow only one answer, others allow multiple
3. **Click Submit** to check your answer
4. The tutor will let you know if you're correct

### Open Answer Questions

Some questions ask you to type your answer:
1. **Read the question** carefully
2. **Type your answer** in the text box
   - Some questions only accept numbers (integers or decimals)
   - Others accept any text
3. **Click Submit** to check your answer
4. The AI evaluates your response and provides feedback

### Ordered List Questions

Some questions ask you to arrange items in the correct order:
1. **Read the question** carefully (e.g., "Put these in order from smallest to largest")
2. **Drag and drop** the items to rearrange them into the correct sequence
3. **Click Submit** to check your order
4. You get credit for each item in the correct position

### Retry Attempts

If you answer incorrectly, the tutor may give you another chance:
- The tutor might provide a hint to help you
- Try a different answer and submit again
- Some quizzes limit the number of attempts

### Quiz Tips

- **Take your time** - read all the options before selecting or typing
- **Listen to hints** - the tutor's guidance can help you find the right answer
- **Don't worry about mistakes** - quizzes are for learning, not just testing
- **For open answer**: The AI understands different phrasings, so focus on the concept

## Tracking Your Progress

### Scores

When you complete a resource, your score is recorded. Scores are displayed with color indicators:
- Green indicates strong performance
- Yellow indicates moderate performance
- Red indicates areas that may need review

### Activity Log

Your dashboard shows a chronological record of your completed resources, including:
- Course name
- Resource name
- Score achieved
- Completion date

## Getting Help

### Contacting Your Teacher

If you have questions about course content or need assistance, you can contact your teacher directly from the dashboard:

1. Find the course you have a question about in your **Course Enrollments** section
2. Click the **three-dot menu** (⋮) on the right side of that course row
3. Select **Contact Teacher**
4. Fill in the subject and your message
5. Click **Send Message**

Your message will be sent directly to your assigned teacher for that course. They can see your progress and will respond to help you.

### Other Support

If you're experiencing technical issues with the platform (login problems, pages not loading, etc.), contact your administrator for assistance.
